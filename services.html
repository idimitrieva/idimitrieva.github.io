Practical, future-ready solutions across risk management, regulation, ESG, and stress testing

Regulatory Compliance

A structured, practical approach to assessing the current state of your regulatory compliance and identifying specific actions

  • Gap Analysis: Assessing your current status against regulatory requirements and market standards
  • Workshops: Interactive sessions with relevant departments (Risk Management, Compliance, Sustainability, Strategy)
  • Roadmap: Prioritized recommendations for action and a timeline for implementation
  • Project Management: Support during implementation

ESG Integration

Banks need Integrated and Data-Driven Approaches

  • Strategy and Governance: Design of strategy related to ESG risks and opportunities and governance
  • Modelling: ESG scenario analysis and ESG risk modelling, Integration of ESG factors into credit risk and stress testing
  • Validation: Independent review of ESG models and methodologies
  • Regulatory Gap Analysis: Support with EBA / ECB supervisory expectations

Scenario Analysis and Stress Testing

End-to-end approach to forward-looking risk intelligence for banks

  • Scenario Design: Transform data into robust stress-testing insights through scenario design.
  • Simulation Engine: Run simulations to evaluate potential outcomes and risks.
  • Decision Support: Provide decision support tools to aid in strategic (capital) planning and risk management.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensure compliance with BCBS, EBA, and ECB regulations.

Steering Risk Culture

Analysing and developing risk culture in a structured and regulatory-compliant manner

  • Risk Culture Screening: Standardized risk and culture survey (value-based and behavior-oriented)
  • Gap Analysis and Benchmarking: Assessing your current status against regulatory requirements and market standards
  • In-depth Qualitative Analysis: Structured interviews and facilitated group discussions to identify key causes and areas of tension
  • Project Management: Prioritized, actionable measures with clear accountability and support during implementation
